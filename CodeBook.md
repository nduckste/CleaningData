The final tidy dataset file meanData.txt contains the average of each variable/feature grouped by activity and subject.

- Subject - volunteers within an age bracket of 19-48 years
- ActivityDesc
	1. WALKING
	1. WALKING_UPSTAIRS
	1. WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
	1. SITTING
	1. STANDING
	1. LAYING
